About the Workshop

The NSS State Level Research Methodology Workshop aims to enhance research aptitude among students across Maharashtra by providing structured training in research methodology, academic writing, presentation, and publication processes.

Objectives
  • Understanding research fundamentals
  • Problem identification and research design
  • Hands-on methodology and tools training
  • Publication guidance
Expected Outcomes
  • Improved research skills
  • Confidence in academic writing
  • Publication-ready research papers

About NSS – SIT Lonavala

National Service Scheme (NSS) at Sinhgad Institute of Technology, Lonavala works with the vision of developing socially responsible students through community engagement, leadership, and research-oriented activities.
